The header status icons in this directory (cf_icon_*.svg, sensor_*.png) are
copied from the Betaflight Configurator project:
https://github.com/betaflight/betaflight-configurator

They are used here for the connection header's sensor/status row (gyro,
accelerometer, magnetometer, barometer, GPS, RC link, rangefinder, arming,
failsafe).
